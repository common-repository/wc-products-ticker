== Installation ==
= From your WordPress dashboard =
* **Step 1:** Visit Plugins > Add New and Search **WooCommerce Products Ticker**
* **Step 2:** Click Install Now button.
* **Step 3:** After Successfully installed the plugin, activate plugin from your plugins page.
* **Step 4:** After activation, go-to widgets area, drag the **WooCommerce Products Ticker** widget onto one of your sidebars and configure its specific settings.

= From FTP =
* **Step 1:** Download **WooCommerce Products Ticker** Zip Folder and Upload the zip folder to your `/wp-content/plugins/ directory.`
* **Step 2:** Activate plugin from your plugins page.
* **Step 3:** After activation, go-to widgets area and drag the **WooCommerce Products Ticker** widget onto one of your sidebars and configure its specific settings.

Enjoy!
